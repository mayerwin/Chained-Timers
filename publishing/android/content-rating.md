# Play Console — Content Rating questionnaire answers

Walk through the questionnaire (Set up your app → Content rating → Start questionnaire). Use these answers verbatim. **All answers are NO** — Chained Timers has no objectionable content.

---

## Step 1: Email

→ Use your developer account email.

## Step 2: Category

→ **Reference, News, or Educational** (closest match — there's no "Tools/Productivity" option, but Reference covers utility apps).

## Step 3: Questions (every category, every question)

### Violence
- Does the app contain violence? → **No**
- Sexual violence? → **No**
- Cartoon, fantasy, or realistic violence? → **No**

### Sexuality
- Does the app contain nudity, sexual content, or sexual references? → **No**
- Does the app contain content related to sexual exploitation? → **No**

### Profanity
- Does the app contain profanity, crude humor, or expletives? → **No**

### Controlled substances
- Does the app reference, depict, or promote drugs, alcohol, or tobacco? → **No**

### Gambling
- Does the app contain gambling, simulated gambling, or in-app gambling content? → **No**

### User-generated content
- Does your app allow users to interact or share content with each other? → **No** (chains are private to each device; export-as-JSON is local file save only)

### Personal info shared
- Does the app share user-provided personal information with anyone? → **No**

### Location sharing
- Does the app share the user's precise location? → **No**

### Digital purchases
- Does the app allow users to make digital purchases? → **No**

### Restricted content (drugs, weapons, hate)
- Does the app contain references to or depictions of controlled drugs, hate groups, weapons, or self-harm? → **No**

### Miscellaneous
- Does the app feature unmoderated user-generated content? → **No**
- Does the app feature mini-games or unlockable content? → **No**
- Does the app feature any content not covered above that may be unsuitable for all ages? → **No**

---

## Step 4: Submit

The questionnaire returns these ratings automatically:

| Authority | Rating |
| --- | --- |
| **IARC Generic** | Everyone (3+) |
| **PEGI** (Europe) | 3 |
| **ESRB** (Americas) | Everyone |
| **ACB** (Australia) | G |
| **GRAC** (Korea) | All |
| **ClassInd** (Brazil) | Livre |

These are the lowest possible ratings — exactly what we want for a fitness/utility app.

Click **Apply rating** to commit. The rating section turns green in the Play Console.
