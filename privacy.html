Privacy Policy Short version: nothing leaves your device.

Last updated: 26 April 2026 · Version 1.0.0

Chained Timers does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data — full stop. No analytics. No crash reporter. No ads. No third-party trackers. No remote config. No account system.

What is stored, and where

All chains, settings, and history are stored locally on your device using the operating system's local storage (browser localStorage for the web/PWA version, native Preferences plugin for the Android and iOS native builds). Nothing is uploaded to any server we operate, and no third-party SDK has access to it.

Network requests

The app makes the following network requests, and only these:

  • Web fonts from fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com on the public website. Google may log standard request metadata (IP, User-Agent) per their privacy policy; we receive none of this. The native Android and iOS builds bundle the necessary fonts and do not contact Google.
  • Initial asset download on first install of the PWA. After that the service worker serves everything offline.

Permissions we request

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS on Android, equivalent on iOS): to display the segment-transition alerts you've configured.
  • Exact alarms (USE_EXACT_ALARM / SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM on Android 12+): to schedule those notifications at the precise times required by your timer chains, even when the screen is off.
  • Vibration (VIBRATE on Android, Haptic feedback on iOS): to vibrate the device at segment transitions, if enabled in settings.
  • Wake lock (WAKE_LOCK on Android, idle timer disabled on iOS): to keep the screen awake during a chain, if you enable that setting.
  • Boot completion (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED on Android): so your scheduled notifications survive a phone restart.

No camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, photo library, or any other personal-data-bearing permission is requested.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from any user, including children, because it does not collect data from anyone. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what the app collects (we currently have no plans to), this page will be updated and the version number above bumped. You can subscribe to the GitHub repository to be notified.

Contact

For questions, please open an issue at github.com/mayerwin/Chained-Timers/issues.
